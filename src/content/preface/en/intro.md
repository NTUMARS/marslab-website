The **Multimodal embodied AI and Robotic Systems (MARS) Lab** at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, is directed by Prof. Jianfei Yang. We study **Physical AI** — building intelligent systems that perceive, understand, reason about, and act in the physical world.

Our research spans embodied AI, robot learning and manipulation, multimodal foundation models, world models for decision-making, and human–robot interaction. We bring together perception, generative modeling, and control to develop robots and embodied agents that are general, reliable, and ready for the real world.
